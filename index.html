About Me

I hold a B.S. in Data Analytics from Purdue University Global, where I achieved a near-perfect GPA of 3.95. My academic and professional journey has been marked by deep involvement in data wrangling, visualization, and model building.

In my professional capacity at Administer Justice, I spearheaded major data initiatives. I designed and implemented a scalable data pipeline, transformed unstructured data into actionable insights, and developed PowerBI dashboards that significantly enhanced decision-making for senior leadership and partners nationwide. This work not only streamlined reporting processes but also saved over 15 hours per week and bolstered customer engagement through an innovative automated communication strategy.

At Calvin University, I directed portfolio management strategies for a $2.8M student endowment fund, where I also mentored a team of analysts. My experiences have endowed me with robust technical skills in SQL, Python, and Azure Synapse Analytics.

I am passionate about leveraging data to drive actionable insights and am actively seeking new opportunities to make a meaningful impact. I'm excited about telling stories with data and contributing to projects that require thoughtful analysis and strategic acumen.
